About

My history of professionalism and computer science

  • 2009-2014

    My Humble Beginnings

    I wrote my first Hello World in Java in Computer Science class at Westfield HS. I continued my education in Computer Science at Southern Virginia University, exploring data structures, agile project development and managing big O complexity.

  • October 2014 - April 2018

    Hard Lessons

    I felt like college wasn't for me, so I left to try and gain some market experience. I certainly found it as general manager of a local Italian restaurant. The experience will follow me for the rest of my life.

  • April 2018 - August 2018

    Transition to Full Stack Developer

    I began at the Coding Dojo in April, and before the end of the first week I realized I had finally found my element. Learning the ins and outs of industry standards and meshing frameworks together to create functional programs was exhiliarating. Every successfully added function, every new language or framework, the endless possibile combinations - I love it.
